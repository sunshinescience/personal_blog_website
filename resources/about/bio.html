My name is Sunshine Abbott. I'm a scientist turned front end developer in Oxford, England.

I completed my BS in geology and MS in geochemistry at the University of California, Los Angeles. Afterwards, I upgraded and expanded a supercomputer for an instrument aboard NASA’s Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter mission. It was amazing to piece together hardware, but I was intrigued by the software side of things...

I moved across the world to get my PhD in Sedimentology at Imperial College, London. During that time, I gained some exposure to Python. This piqued my interest in coding and subsequently led me to learn more programming languages.

I’m currently in a Udacity nanodegree program for front end web development.

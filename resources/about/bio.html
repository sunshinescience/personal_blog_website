My name is Sunshine Abbott. I'm an American and a front end developer.

During my BS degree, I majored in geology, then geochemistry (MS degree), and lastly sedimentology (PhD). In between my MS and PhD degrees I was fortunate to have gotten a job as a hardware specialist for the Principal Investigator of the Diviner Lunar Radiometer Experiment, an instrument aboard NASA’s Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter mission. I had the privilege of working on a great team with an awesome supervisor. I upgraded and expanded their supercomputer. It was amazing to learn about the hardware. But I must admit, the software side of things really intrigued me...

I moved across the world to get my PhD in Sedimentology at Imperial College, London. During my PhD, I gained exposure to some coding (Python). The project piqued my interest in coding and led me to transition into the tech sector.

I’m currently in a Udacity nanodegree program for front end web development.
